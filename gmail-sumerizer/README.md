## 📌 Why This Project? (Problem vs. Solution)

### 🛑 The Problem: Email Overload & Data Silos
Managing a high volume of incoming operational or informational emails introduces several friction points for modern workflows:
* **Information Overload:** Sifting through long, wordy emails (like daily digests, updates, or team notifications) manually wastes valuable developer and communication time.
* **Lack of Visibility:** Crucial operational updates remain trapped inside individual or team inboxes, making it difficult for the rest of the team to track trends or reference past updates without manual searching.
* **Manual Entry Errors:** Manually copying email summaries, sender details, and key metrics into tracking systems or databases is tedious, slow, and prone to human error.

### 💡 The Solution: Automated AI-Powered Archiving
This n8n workflow completely automates the ingestion, analysis, and structured logging of incoming emails by connecting a **Gmail Trigger**, an **AI Agent**, and **Google Sheets**:
* **Instant Context at a Glance:** The workflow listens for new emails in real-time. Instead of requiring someone to read the whole text, the integrated **AI Agent** extracts the core subject matter, key points, and critical details instantly.
* **Centralized Data Hub:** The AI-generated summaries—along with metadata like the sender's email address, subject line, and specific category labels—are automatically mapped and appended as clean rows in a Google Sheet.
* **Team Transparency & Automation:** By moving raw inbox data into a structured spreadsheet, the data becomes universally accessible for team review, future reporting, or triggering secondary automated workflows downstream.
